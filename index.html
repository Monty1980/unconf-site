The Intersection Unconference

Welcome

An unconference is a creation of the people who attend. This free invite-only event — hosted by Square — brings together the people we know and admire, and the people we want to know better. At Intersection, you can expect a diverse mix of 75 entrepreneurs, hackers, program creators, industry innovators, academic leaders, technology pioneers, professors, and researchers.

We provide the theme—how academic and industry can partner and influence each other—and you decide what to talk about and what to share.

Getting to Square

Public Transportation
We recommend taking Muni to the Van Ness Station, or BART to the Civic Center Station. Both stations are just a few minutes walk from Square.
Biking Square offers on-site bike parking for visitors. Enter with your bike through the 1455 Market Street entrance. A front desk security guard will escort you to guest bike parking. Driving We recommend parking in the Civic Center Plaza Garage. It’s located at 355 McAllister Street, between Polk and Larkin, and offers hourly parking at the driver’s expense. Arriving at Square Be sure to bring a government-issued ID. When you arrive, please check in with the front desk security team. Let them know you are here to visit Square. After checking in, proceed to the 9th floor for opening night. Wi-Fi Network: Square-Guest Password: fieldinternet
